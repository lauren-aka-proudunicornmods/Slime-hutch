Choose the version you would prefer and copy to your mods folder

A = 6 sprinklers
B = 12 sprinklers
C = 24 sprinklers

They come with either 1 seed maker or no seed makers.
They are available after day four of the game, if you have enough hearts with the NPCs who will send you the letters.

After you have received all the items, you can delete the mod if you like.

Version D = a request
6 sprinklers, 3 seed makers, no heart point requirements, available day 1.
